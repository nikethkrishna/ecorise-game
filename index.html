ECORISE
RESTORE THE FALLEN CITY | 3D SIMULATION
Factories poisoned the sky, sewage cut through every district, and the city forgot what clean water looked like. You are the restoration lead. Rebuild neighborhoods, heal the land, and turn a dying industrial grid back into a living city.

THE LORE

EcoRise begins after years of unchecked industrial expansion. The air is toxic, rivers are collapsing, and citizens survive behind masks. Your job is to reverse the damage before the city becomes permanent wasteland.

OBJECTIVES

Purify the air and push the skyline back to blue.
Clean the water, unlock rivers, and restore ponds across the map.
Rebuild homes, services, and civic life so the city can thrive again.

HOW TO PLAY

Select buildings from the bottom bar or press keys `1-8` to place them.
Use `D` to arm demolition charges, click dark runoff streams to clean them, and scroll to zoom.
Use the minimap and side panels to jump around the city. Missions only appear after the game starts.
